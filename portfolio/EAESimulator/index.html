EAE Simulator

September 2018
position: Programmer & Designer & Artist


Overview

EAE Simulater is a pixel-style game that simulates a game academy, which is a prototype made in one week. You are the founder of a game academy with a bunch of money. You need to use your money and reputation to make the academy to be the No.1 game program. You can do lots of things such as recruiting professors and students, setting classes, building the lab, getting internship and research oppotunities for students, and networking. You must keep the balance of everything while developing.

My initial idea of designing this game is to make people realize us and reveal some realistic problems in the game academies. But I also want it to be a relaxing game, and players can have fun in simulating.

What I Did

  • Designed all concepts of the demo including core mechanics, art style, and game value balance.
  • Implemented the main game loop, tile-based environment, and events in Unreal.
  • Implemented data-driven UI system.
