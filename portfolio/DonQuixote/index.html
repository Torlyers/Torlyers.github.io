Don Quixote in Manhattan

September 2017
position: Programmer & Designer & Artist


Overview

Don Quixote in Manhattan is an action game prototype I made in one week. Don Quixote is the lonely knight of the big city, fighting for ensuring his own existence. He's Sisyphus, Don Quixote, and anyone. I want to make an experience that people feel lonely in a big city, overwhelmed by skyscrapers, but can still insist on their faith.
On the action side, Don Quixote is a knight with his sword and horse, which is not suitable for Manhattan. So I designed a motorbike for him/her. The ability of driving on walls can make it more magic realistic.

What I Did


  • Designed all concepts of the demo including narrative and gameplay mechanics.
  • Implemented gameplay logic in Unity.
  • Made character animations and blend. Made path movement animations.
  • Using plug-ins and to make the motorbike movement logic and hair physics.
  • Imported art assets and adjust them to in the same style.
