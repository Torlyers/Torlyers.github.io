The Journey to the West: Super Power

February 2016 - August 2016
position: game designer



An online mobile ARPG about Journey to the West with a brand-new story. The stage is transferred into future and heroes have their new weapons. Players can use various kinds of guns and skills to defeat enemies.

I participated in this program as a level designer & production intern in Leiting Studio, NetEase, which is the second biggest commercial game company in China. I am in charge of designing and producting all the related contents in several levels including monsters, battles, QTEs, and some art assets such as 3D models, animations, and particle effects. About an specific monster, I designed its skills and AI. The design of the skill includes both its appearance and its math values like damage and attacking range. The vedio includes some levels and monsters I designed. The program is still on developing.

I worked with an experienced developing team and cooperated with programmers, artists, and other designers. This experience improved my abality of cooperating and deepened the comprehension about commercial game progarm development pipeline
