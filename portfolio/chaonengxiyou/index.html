The Journey to the West: Super Power

February 2016 - August 2016
position: game designer


overview

An online mobile ARPG about Journey to the West with a brand-new story. The stage is transferred into future and heroes have their new weapons. Players can use various kinds of guns and skills to defeat enemies and clear levels.

What I Did

  • Built battle levels of the game independently, deploying enemies and controlling the battle rhythm
  • Designed monsters by calculating battle values, developing AI, and designing their skills.
  • Designed art resources such as QTE animations and particle effect.
  • Collaborated with programmers to produce systems and functions of the game.
