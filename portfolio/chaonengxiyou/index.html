The Journey to the West: Super Power

February 2016 - August 2016
position: game designer



An online mobile ARPG about Journey to the West with a brand-new story. The stage is transferred into future and heroes have their new weapons. Players can use various kinds of guns and skills to defeat enemies.

I participate in this program as a game designer intern in Leiting Studio, NetEase, which is the biggest commercial game company in China. I am in charge of designing all the contents including monsters, battles, QTE and some art design like actions and effects of several discrete missions. About an certain monster, I design its skills(appearance and quantitative value)and AI. The vedio includes some parts of my missions. The program is still developing.

I worked with an experienced developing team and cooperated with programmers and artists, which enhanced my abality of cooperation and comprehension about commercial progarm development
