Open Ocean

October 2017
position: programmer

overview

Open Ocean is a game about collecting treasure and pushing your luck. This game was took from Lunar Lander of Atari in 1979. We tried to find a way to iterating on it. Because landing in a low gravity is the core gameplay of the original game, we decided to switch the environment to the deep ocean, and let players to control a submarine to land, which is quite like landing on the lunar land. Besides, we made a more complex terrain and add some treasures to give players more positive feedbacks.

what i did

I developed the first version by Phaser, a web game framework. In that version, I implemented gameplay logic and some necessary functions like polygon collision and tween animations. You can play web version here.

To develop a mobile version, the next iteration is made by Unity. I added touch screen interaction, gravity interaction, and score board. Spritesheet Animations and tween animations are also optimized.
