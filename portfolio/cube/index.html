Cube

April 2016 - June 2016
position: game designer & programmer & artist


overview

A cube's journey to find emotions. Levels were designed according to emotions like joy, sadness, and fear. The design is to build a world of cubes where characters, scenes and controllers are all cubes. Players can contral the cube to traverse the scene, avoid traps and reach the terminal point. I was the main designer of game, designing the gameplay, hardware and a mission. Besides, I implemented the hardware part and did some visual optimizing.

what i did

I designed a controller based on Arduino. The controller is a cube that can synchronize with the cube in the game. I used gyroscope and magnetic flux sensor to implement the function and used 3mm board to assemble a shell. The controller connects to computer by bluetooth, transporting data to serial port.
