Cube

May 2016 - June 2016
position: game designer & programmer & artist

The trip of a cube to find emotion. The missions were designed on some emotions like joy, sadness and fear. My thinking is to construct a world of cubes where character, scenes and controllers are all cubes. Players can contral the cube to traverse the scene, avoid traps and reach the terminal point. I was the main designer of game, designing the gameplay, hardware and a mission. Besides, I implemented the hardware part and did some visual optimizing.

I designed a controller based on Arduino. The controller is a cube that can synchronize with the cube in the game. I used gyroscope and magnetic flux sensor to implement the function and used 3mm board to assemble a shell. The controller connects to computer by bluetooth, transporting data to serial port.

The game can also be played on keyboard. Use direction key to move, space to jump and press 'z' and 'x' to choose facing direction.
