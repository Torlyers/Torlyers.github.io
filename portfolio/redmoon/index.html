overview

The Red Moon is a multi-player digital board game which is built on Android platform. Once every two years, when shadows devour the moon, the Guǐ become confident and unruly. As the leader of your city, you must defend against the Guǐ, and ensure your city’s prosperity. The game is about Town Building, Alliances, and Magic. Defeat Guǐ. Siege other cities. Cast powerful spells. And most of all, beware the red moon!
