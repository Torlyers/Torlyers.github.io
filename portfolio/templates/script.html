No.7 Domitory

November 2015


No.7 Domitory is a script. It tells a story happened in the domitory of my university. The character Si always has bad dreams and feels heartache. To find the reason, he cooperate with his roommate Dr.Hou. In the process, they find that the dream is related to an event happened 50 years ago in No.7 Domitory.

The script was written in Chinese. It contains some Chinese element like yin-yang principle and daoist magic. The numbers of words is about 10 thousand.
